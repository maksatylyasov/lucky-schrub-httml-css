About Us

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of". Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Philodendron (Heartleaf)

This classic houseplant features heart-shaped leaves that trail elegantly from a hanging basket or climb up a trellis. It's easy to care for and can adapt to a variety of lighting conditions, making it a popular choice for any home.

Snake Plant (Sansevieria trifasciata)

This hardy plant, also known as mother-in-law's tongue, is a great choice for beginners. Its striking upright leaves come in a variety of patterns and colors, making it a popular choice for modern decor.

Pilea Peperomioides (Chinese Money Plant)

This trendy plant features round, coin-shaped leaves that are said to bring good luck and prosperity. Its compact size makes it a great choice for small spaces, and it's easy to care for, making it a favorite among new plant parents.

Monstera Deliciosa (Swiss Cheese Plant)

This large, tropical plant is a showstopper with its glossy, perforated leaves. It's a great choice for adding a touch of jungle-inspired decor to any space, and it's relatively easy to care for.
